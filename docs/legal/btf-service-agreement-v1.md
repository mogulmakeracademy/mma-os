## 1. SCOPE OF SERVICES

MMA agrees to provide the BUILD-to-FUND ("BTF") program over a 90-day period beginning on the Effective Date. The BTF program is divided into three phases:

**Phase 1 — BUILD (Days 1-30):** Entity verification, foundational compliance audit, business address optimization, business credit bureau file opening, business banking setup guidance.

**Phase 2 — STACK (Days 30-60):** Strategic tradeline sequencing playbook, business credit bureau scoring optimization, vendor net-30 setup guidance, utilization strategy.

**Phase 3 — FUND (Days 60-90):** Lender matching by entity profile, funding product matching (LOC vs. term vs. equipment vs. invoice factoring), application sequencing strategy, pre-application file review.

**Application submission:** MMA does NOT submit applications to lenders on behalf of Client during the 90-day program. Application execution is the Client's responsibility, and MMA provides only the strategy, sequencing, and pre-flight file review. Applications may begin AFTER Day 90 only when Client has completed all required action items.

### 1.1 What MMA Will Do (Deliverables)
- 90 days of coach access through the client portal
- Audit of Client Entity for fundability compliance (entity status, NAICS code, address compliance, EIN verification)
- Pull and review of Dun & Bradstreet, Experian Business, and Equifax Business credit files
- Strategic recommendations and step-by-step playbooks for Client to execute
- Educational content (15-email education sequence + access to MMA classroom + future LaunchPad tool access)
- Document review (entity formation docs, banking statements, EIN letters)
- Lender shortlist customized to Client's entity profile and funding goals
- Application sequencing strategy and pre-application review
- Coach messages in the client portal throughout the program
- Phase advancement tracking with milestone-based check-ins

### 1.2 What MMA Will NOT Do
- **Submit credit or funding applications on behalf of Client during the 90-day program.** Application execution is Client's responsibility.
- **Guarantee approval of any application.** No outcome is guaranteed. Approval depends on Client's execution, lender criteria, and factors outside MMA's control.
- **Guarantee a specific dollar amount of funding.** Any references to typical outcomes are not promises or projections of Client's specific results.
- **Provide legal advice.** MMA is not a law firm. While our work touches on business entity compliance, Client should consult a licensed attorney for legal matters.
- **Provide tax advice.** MMA is not a CPA firm. Client should consult a licensed tax professional for tax matters.
- **Provide financial planning or investment advice.** MMA does not advise on investment strategy.
- **Open bank accounts on Client's behalf.** Banking institutions require in-person or institution-specific signup processes that Client must complete.
- **Act as a credit repair organization.** MMA does not dispute, remove, or modify items on Client's personal or business credit reports. Client may engage a separate licensed credit repair service if needed.
