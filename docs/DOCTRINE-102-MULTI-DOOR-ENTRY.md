# Doctrine §102 — Multi-Door Entry Model
# Effective: 2026-06-28 per Antonio direct
# Status: CANONICAL. Applies to all docs, agents, partner materials, ads, sales scripts.

## The Rule

**Do NOT describe the PME funnel as a linear sequence.** Customers enter through MULTIPLE INDEPENDENT DOORS and may adopt only ONE product across their entire relationship with us. Treat products as PARALLEL, not SEQUENTIAL.

## Top of Funnel (TOFU)

Social media (Instagram, YouTube, TikTok, Threads, LinkedIn, Facebook) + podcast appearances + paid ads + organic content + referrals + Antonio's personal brand.

TOFU is upstream of EVERY entry door below. A single piece of social content might drive someone to ANY of the doors — or to none.

## Entry Doors (parallel, not sequential)

A prospect can enter through any of:

  1. **The Launch Pad** — Free 2-min Fundability Check, then $19 or $47/mo if they paywall-convert. They may never join the community or buy BTF.
  2. **MMA Skool community** — Free or by referral from existing members. They may never subscribe to Launch Pad or buy BTF.
  3. **Live free workshops** — Workshop Wednesday, Coffee Hour, special events. They may attend repeatedly, never subscribe to anything.
  4. **Paid tiers directly** — Premium ($44/mo) or VIP ($97/mo) without going through Free first if they're already convinced.
  5. **BUILD-to-FUND directly** — High-intent prospects can buy $4,997 BTF without ever subscribing to Launch Pad or joining community.
  6. **Referrals** — Existing customers refer new prospects to ANY door above.
  7. **Future: Paige Agent AI** — Multi-business operators will enter directly into Paige when public.

## What customers MAY do (any combination is valid)

  - Use only Launch Pad ($19/mo) forever — valid.
  - Use only Skool Free → Premium → VIP, never touch Launch Pad — valid.
  - Attend free workshops only, never subscribe — valid (still in the audience).
  - Skip Launch Pad entirely, go Free Skool → BTF — valid.
  - Go ads → BTF $4,997 directly with no prior touchpoint — valid.
  - Use Launch Pad AND MMA Premium AND eventually buy BTF — valid (most expansion-friendly).

There is NO "right path." There is only the path that fits the customer.

## What this means for partners + agents + ad copy

  - **NEVER** position any single product as "the on-ramp to" any other product.
  - **NEVER** say "you have to go through X before Y."
  - **DO** describe products as independent value propositions that happen to compose well.
  - **DO** route prospects to the door that fits their stated need RIGHT NOW.
  - **DO** mention adjacent products as options, not requirements.

## What this means for funnel diagrams

Stop drawing the funnel as a single waterfall (TOFU -> MOFU -> BOFU sequentially through specific tiers). Draw it as:

  - A pool of TOFU sources at the top
  - Multiple parallel entry doors below
  - Lateral arrows showing optional cross-pollination between products
  - Customer may stop at ANY product and that's a complete journey

## What this means for sales scripts

When a prospect asks "where do I start?", the answer is:
  - "Where do you want to be in 90 days?" (then route to the product that gets them there)
  - NOT "everyone starts with Free Skool" or "everyone takes the Fundability Check first"

## Doctrines this corrects/supersedes

  - §99 (BTF Canon) — funnel description was overly linear. BTF can be entered directly.
  - §100 (Revenue Ladder) — the term "ladder" implies sequence. Rename mentally to "Revenue Menu" or "Revenue Pool." Customers don't climb a ladder; they pick from a menu.
  - §101 (Three Revenue Streams) — accurate on the streams; needed the multi-door framing layered on top.

## Codified examples (archetype-only per Doctrine §116)

GOOD: "A workshop attendee closed BTF directly. They didn't go through Launch Pad and aren't currently in the community."

BAD: "Every BTF client comes from the community first, then Fundability Check, then BTF in sequence."

GOOD: "We have multiple entry points so prospects can find us through whatever channel fits them — social, workshops, referrals, ads, direct search."

BAD: "Our funnel is Free Skool -> Workshop Wed -> BTF."

## Bottom line

PME runs a portfolio of independent products that compose well but don't require each other. Never let any doc, agent, or partner imply otherwise.
