## Body

Hey {{first_name}},

Question I get all the time: "Which bank should I use for my business?" Wrong question.

**The bank matters less than HOW you use the account.** Lenders score FIVE things, and the bank brand is not one:

**1. Account age** - 6-month-old account >> 6-week-old. Start the clock day one.

**2. Average daily balance** - Not statement-date balance. AVERAGE across the period. Funding 3 days before payroll then draining does not fool the algorithm.

**3. Deposit frequency + consistency** - Daily deposits = real business. Sporadic big deposits = personal transfers laundered through business. Same volume, very different score.

**4. NSF count** - Even ONE NSF in last 90 days kills most applications.

**5. Tied to a REAL business account** - Not a personal account converted to business. Bank statement metadata tells the story.

**Funding ceiling impact:**
Account with 6 months history + $5K avg daily balance + daily deposits + zero NSFs + opened clean as business = approval ceiling roughly **2-3x higher** than thin profile.
