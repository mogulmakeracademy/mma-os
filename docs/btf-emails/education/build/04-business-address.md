Underwriters have software that flags business addresses in 5 seconds.

**Red flag #1: UPS Store / mailbox forwarding** - Tagged CMRA (Commercial Mail Receiving Agency). Auto-decline at many banks. **Fix:** Real virtual office with phone + suite.

**Red flag #2: Residential address** - Cross-checked against USPS residential records. Screams "side hustle." **Fix:** Commercial address even if virtual.

**Red flag #3: Address mismatch across records** - Articles say one, EIN says another, bank says a third. Auto-flag for fraud review. **Fix:** Pick one. Update EVERYTHING.

**Red flag #4: Out-of-state operating without disclosure** - Filed in Delaware but operating in Texas? Need foreign qualification. Missing it = hiding something. **Fix:** Foreign qualify in operating state.

**Red flag #5: P.O. Box anywhere** - 80%+ business lenders auto-decline. They cannot legally serve papers to a PO box. **Fix:** Never use a PO box. Ever.

Address layer is checked BEFORE credit layer. All bureau work is wasted if address triggers auto-decline before credit pull.
